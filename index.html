The Birla Institute of Technology & Science, BITS Pilani is an all-India Institute for higher education. The primary motive of BITS is to "train young men and women able and eager to create and put into action such ideas, methods, techniques and information".

Department of Chemical Engineering at BITS-Pilani, Pilani Campus is one of the premier departments in the country that provides a unique educational and research environment. We have a major emphasis on interdisciplinary and industrial collaborations.

EXTROPY 2k17, the first edition of the most awaited Chemical Engineering Extravaganza of BITS PILANI, Pilani Campus, is en route to a pectacular launch, in the month of Feb, 2017. Encasing within, a plethora of events and activities, quizzes and competitions, EXTROPY promises to envisage a number of innovations, ideas and possibilities, that lay forth in the field of Chemical and Industrial Engineering. As the name suggests, EXTROPY focuses on the idea of generating order, progression, structured and organized functioning and opposition to the tendency of randomness in the world, with an aid of cultural and technological developments upcoming in the field of Chemical Engineering!
